# ReBooked Solutions: Standard Seller Pickup Flow Guide

For normal (non-business) sellers on ReBooked Solutions, the **Physical Pickup** fulfillment method is designed to be highly secure, leveraging a **Dual Confirmation** escrow system to protect both parties. Below is a detailed breakdown of how the process works from purchase to payment release.

---

## 1. Purchase & Seller Commitment
1. **Buyer Checkout**: A buyer selects the "Physical Pickup" delivery option and pays through the platform (securely held in escrow via BobPay).
2. **Commit Window**: The seller receives a notification and must log in to "Commit to Sale" within **48 hours**. 
   * *If the seller fails to commit within 48 hours, the order is automatically cancelled, and the buyer is refunded in full (including the Buyer Protection Fee).*

---

## 2. Scheduling & Chat Coordination (7-Day Expiry)
1. **Meeting Setup**: Once the seller commits, the 7-day fulfillment window begins. The buyer and seller must coordinate a meeting spot and time using the secure in-app **Chat**.
2. **Safety Guidelines**:
   * Exchange must take place in a **safe, public, well-lit location** (e.g., shopping center, school grounds, police station parking lot) during daylight hours.
   * Bring a friend or family member if possible.
   * Do not share personal phone numbers or meet in private/isolated spaces.

---

## 3. Handover & Dual Confirmation Escrow
1. **Item Inspection**: At the meeting, the buyer must inspect the item's condition, pages, spine, or authenticity *before* completing the handover.
2. **Dual Confirmation**: 
   * **Seller Action**: The seller clicks "Confirm Handover" on their dashboard when they hand over the item.
   * **Buyer Action**: The buyer clicks "Confirm Receipt" on their dashboard when they receive the item.
   * **Escrow Release**: Funds are **only** released from escrow and credited to the seller's wallet once **both** actions are logged in the database.

---

## 4. Unresolved or Expired Handover Rules
* **7-Day Expiry**: If the 7-day window lapses without dual confirmation:
  * **Auto-Cancellation**: If neither party has raised a dispute, the order is automatically cancelled. The buyer receives a refund of the item price (the R20 Buyer Protection Fee remains non-refundable as platform services were rendered).
  * **Active Dispute**: If one party claims the handover occurred but the other disagrees, either party can click "Report Issue / File Dispute" before the 7-day timer expires to freeze the escrow funds for administrator review.

---

## 5. Post-Handover Protection
* Once Dual Confirmation is logged, the transaction is marked as complete.
* However, the buyer still retains access to the standard **Dispute Process** (Section 10) to raise concerns regarding latent defects or misrepresentations (e.g., missing workbook pages not visible during brief inspection). 
* *Note: Platform protection covers transaction records only and does not extend to personal safety or conduct during the meeting.*
